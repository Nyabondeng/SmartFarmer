About Us

Who We Are

Smart Farmer is a digital agricultural information system designed specifically for smallholder farmers in South Sudan. It provides crop information, educational modules, and a planting log that works offline on any device.

Mission

To provide accessible farming information to smallholder farmers in Yei County, South Sudan, in their own languages, Arabic, English, and Bari without requiring internet access.

Vision

A future where every farmer in South Sudan has the knowledge and tools to grow more food, reduce crop losses, and improve their family's food security.

What Smart Farmer Offers

  • Crop information for 5 staple crops in 3 languages
  • Educational modules on planting, pest control and harvesting
  • Voice output for low-literacy farmers
  • Offline planting log saved on the farmer's device
