Fertilizer Use

Feed your crops the right way for healthy growth and better yields.

Fertilizer Types

Natural Fertilizers

Natural fertilizers are the best option for most farmers in South Sudan. They are cheap, available, and good for the soil.

  • ✓ Compost: Made from decomposed organic matter (kitchen waste, leaves, grass)
  • ✓ Animal manure: Cow, goat, sheep, or chicken manure
  • ✓ Green manure: Plant cover crops and plough them into the soil
  • ✓ Ash: Wood ash contains potassium and other nutrients
Farmer's Tip:

Compost is the most complete natural fertilizer. It adds organic matter and nutrients to the soil.

When to Apply

Applying fertilizer at the right time is important.

  • ✓ Apply compost or manure before planting
  • ✓ Apply nitrogen fertilizer when plants are growing (2-4 weeks after planting)
  • ✓ Apply phosphorus and potassium at planting time
  • ✓ Do not apply fertilizer during the dry season
Farmer's Tip:

Apply fertilizer when the soil is moist. This helps it dissolve and reach the roots.

How to Apply

How you apply fertilizer matters as much as what you use.

  • ✓ Mix fertilizer into the top 10-15cm of soil
  • ✓ Place fertilizer near the roots, not on the leaves
  • ✓ Spread fertilizer evenly across the field
  • ✓ Water after applying fertilizer to help it absorb
Farmer's Tip:

Do not put fertilizer directly on the plant stem. It can burn and damage the plant.

Avoid Over-Fertilizing

Too much fertilizer can harm your crops and waste money.

  • ✓ More is not always better
  • ✓ Too much nitrogen causes weak stems and attracts pests
  • ✓ Over-fertilizing can burn roots
  • ✓ Use only what your crops need
Farmer's Tip:

A soil test is the best way to know what your soil needs. Ask your local extension officer for help.

Watering After Fertilizer

Water helps fertilizer reach the roots.

  • ✓ Water the field after applying fertilizer
  • ✓ Watering helps the fertilizer dissolve and go down to the roots
  • ✓ Do not apply fertilizer if no rain is expected

Summary

  • ✓ Use natural fertilizers like compost and manure
  • ✓ Apply at the right time and in the right way
  • ✓ Do not over-fertilize
  • ✓ Water after applying fertilizer
